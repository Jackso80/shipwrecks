A Little about the project

We based our project off of data colected by the New Jersey Maratime Museum. The dataset starts in the 18th century and covers some 4000 individual incidents, mostly spanning the new jersey coast, but with a few in the greater new jersey area.

Looking at the dataset and doing some intial investigation into the dataset we found a spike around the mid 19th century which we couldn't attribute to anything particularly obvious. It started during the civil war and continued afterward, indicating it was not specfically related to the war, or linked to some postwar economic boom. This inspired us to look a little deeper into this surprsing uptick in wrecks. This is when we came across information about ship technologies at the time. This eventually led us to follow the trail of ship design in the era and we found it linked pretty closely to the time frame for novel ship innovations.
